Hey, I’m Karen.
Nice to meet you.

I’ve been working as a graphic designer in the fashion industry for 2 years, after graduating from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo with a degree in Graphic Communication. I believe design-thinking is important. With this as a priority, I completed UCI's UX/UI bootcamp under their Division of Continuing Education from April to October 2020.



I enjoy working and collaborating on teams, designing for clients, and carrying on a vision. A lot of my work is inspired by the sneaker and streetwear industry, which is the industry I hope to break into. When I’m not designing, I enjoy spending my time playing Smash Bros, eating fusion street food, or following innovative fashion brands.
